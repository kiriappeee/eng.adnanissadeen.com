Welcome to My Little Engineering Corner

Hi! My name is Adnan Issadeen and I'm a systems engineer at Buffer

This is my little corner of the internet where I share logs, thoughts, and other various notes related to the programming projects that I'm working on on a daily basis. This will hopefully include the work that I do at Buffer if I can get a go ahead for it.

Outisde of the work I do at Buffer, I work on projects ranging from evaluating and trying out frameworks/tools/cloud platforms, to working on coding challenges like Project Euler, or Advent of Code. I also work on side projects trying to build little products in the form of very small web apps or simple browser extensions.

From time to time there'll be blog posts about new projects I'm starting on, and thoughts on tools and processes in the industry. Expect an emphasis on TDD and containers (I think we are long past the stage where one groans upon reading or hearing that word. :D )

I've also gotten permission to talk about the work I do at Buffer which is a big deal. This could provide an insight into what it is like to work in a small company like Buffer which services a pretty darn big customer base.

My hope is that this will prove to be an educational journey eventually. I've wanted to be a teacher for the longest while and I'm hoping this can be the perfect blend of writing software for a living combined with teaching. I remember starting out in the software world wondering where on earth do I start picking up things from. Even now, I feel like I don't have a grasp of anything! If I can help someone else who's never met me, purely through what I write here, even if it's one person, then it'll all be worth it.
